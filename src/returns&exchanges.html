Returns & Exchanges

Return Policy

We hope you love your purchase, but if you are not completely satisfied, we offer a simple returns policy. You can return most new, unopened items within 30 days of delivery for a full refund or exchange.

  • Timeframe for Returns: 30 days from the date of delivery.
  • Condition of Items: Items must be in their original condition, unused, and in the original packaging.
  • Return Process: To initiate a return, please contact our customer support team at support@kitikoreanstore.com or call +91 234 567 890.
  • Return Shipping Fees: Customers are responsible for the return shipping fees unless the item is defective or incorrect.

Exchange Policy

If you would like to exchange an item for a different size, color, or another product, we are happy to assist. Exchanges are subject to product availability.

  • Timeframe for Exchanges: Within 30 days of receiving your order.
  • Condition of Items: Items must be in their original condition, unused, and in the original packaging.
  • Exchange Process: To initiate an exchange, please contact our customer support team at support@kitikoreanstore.com or call +91 234 567 890.
  • Exchange Shipping Fees: If the exchange is due to a defective or wrong item, we will cover the shipping costs. Otherwise, customers are responsible for return shipping fees.

Refund Policy

Once we receive and inspect your returned items, we will process your refund. Refunds will be credited back to your original method of payment.

  • Refund Time: Refunds typically take 5-7 business days to process after receiving the returned items.
  • Original Payment Method: Refunds will be issued to the original payment method used for the purchase.
  • Partial Refunds: In some cases, partial refunds may be granted for used or damaged items.
